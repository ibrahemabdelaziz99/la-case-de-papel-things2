The Story

A mysterious man called (the professor) is preparing for the largest theft operation ever accomplished, by recruiting 8 people who claim the names of the movement are the names of global cities, and these people have criminal precedents and wanted security and they have nothing to lose. The idea is to break into the Spanish minting house and print 2.4 billion euros and they need to achieve that to stay there 11 days and deal with 67 hostages and elite Spanish police forces. The story of the series begins with the awakening of Tokyo from a dream and her path to her mother to contact with her to meet and deposit her, before she traveled to China where she is wanted by the police, and without knowing the police were listening to the call and planning to detain her, and on her way to her mother's house, which was ambushed to arrest her, a red car stands and talks with her A mysterious man persuaded her not to go, which she later called the Guardian Angel, to offer her a job which is stealing 2.4 billion euros. Tokyo agrees and goes with him to a country house with eight people who will spend five months in that house to train to steal a printing house for money in Spain.
